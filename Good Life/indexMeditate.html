Meditate regulary start in the mourning when you wake up. You are probably already laying down with your eyes shut, use a few minutes to clear your mind. Use medition to explore what you really want in life. Avoid busy work. It takes time from more important things, leaving less for family and whatever activities you want to work on. What you want in life may require skills. Skills are gained through study and practice.

  • To begin find a quiet place that fills you with calm.
  • Listen to your breathing and heartbeat.
  • Close your eyes and attempt to clear your mind.
  • Be positve and reject negativity.
  • Finish with loving supportive thoughts.
