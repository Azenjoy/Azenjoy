Make an effort to list a few things you are thankful for everyday. A routine of gratitude journaling may sound cheesy but cheese is good. It will make you feel better to think about a few things you are happy about rather than focusing only on complaints.

  • Start each entry with a statement of gratitude.
  • Write down a few things you are grateful for each day.
  • Reflect on how those things make you feel and why you are thankful for them.
  • Include descriptions of the people, places, and experiences that bring you joy.
  • Take time to reflect on your entries and how they make you feel throughout the day.
  • Use your journal to track any positive changes in your life that have come from practicing gratitude regularly.
  • Share your gratitude with others by writing thank-you notes or expressing appreciation in person or online.
